    Music Jukebox

    In this task the requirement was to create a multimedia application for an online music jukebox. The application had to include the following; HTML5, CSS3, PHP6 and MySQL to form a website viewable on a desktop. Our module leader allowed the use of W3Schools templates to help aid our site building understanding.

    Using examples such as BBC radio desktop application and MTV music charts player helped me understand what all users want to know. I included the main elements such as; the artists name, song and the album this can be seen in (photo 1). The usability is simple, once the users click on the song of their choice the page the user lands on will display the picture of the album cover, artist and album title. Along with all this is a music player that allows the user to play, pause, scrub through and change the volume of the song.

    0:12 - User lands on the homepage and begins to scroll through.
    0:18 - Welcome introduction to the site can be seen and little after that the main content of the site is shown being the jukebox list.
    0:42 - User selects a song.
    0:43 - User lands on music player page.
    0:50 - User begins to play music and manipulate with music player controls.
